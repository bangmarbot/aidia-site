Use Case — Online Travel Agent

OTA Workforce Trends
2025-2026

How AI is reshaping the travel industry workforce — and what it means for HR.

📊 Key Context

The OTA industry has fundamentally shifted post-pandemic.

  • OTA industry recovered post-COVID but shifted to lean, tech-driven operations
  • Major layoffs: Booking.com (~2,800), Expedia (~1,500), Traveloka & Tiket.com streamlined
  • Root cause: over-hiring during recovery + AI automation replacing manual roles
  • OTAs are now tech companies selling travel, not travel companies using tech

Declining vs Growing Roles

Declining Roles

  • Manual booking & reservation agents
  • Traditional call center representatives
  • Manual data entry operators
  • Offline marketing teams
  • Back-office admin (paper-based)

Growing Roles

  • AI/ML Engineers — personalization, pricing
  • Data Scientists — forecasting, dynamic pricing
  • Product Managers (AI-focused)
  • Content Automation & SEO Specialists
  • Revenue Management Analysts

🤖 AI Transformation in OTAs

How AI is replacing traditional roles

  • Customer service: AI chatbots replaced 80%+ of tier-1 support (Expedia, Kayak use ChatGPT)
  • Agent-to-customer ratio shifted from 1:200 to 1:2,000+ with AI assistance
  • Dynamic pricing: ML algorithms optimize prices in real-time — no more manual pricing teams
  • Content: AI generates hotel descriptions, travel guides, SEO content at scale
  • Personalization: recommendation engines replace manual curation teams

🇮🇩 Indonesia OTA Landscape

Traveloka
Pivoted to fintech (PayLater) — hiring more fintech talent than travel operations
Tiket.com
Lean team, tech-driven operations. Acquired by Blibli/Djarum ecosystem
PegiPegi
Significantly downsized operations
Trend: Less operations headcount, more tech/product/data roles. The shift is irreversible.

💡 Implications for HR & Workforce Planning

  • Reskill existing staff: customer service → AI supervision, data analysis
  • Hire for adaptability, not just domain expertise
  • Invest in AI literacy across all departments — not just engineering
  • Build human-AI hybrid teams: AI does volume, humans do judgment

🎯 Key Takeaway

"The future OTA workforce is small, technical, and AI-augmented. Humans handle strategy, partnerships, and edge cases. AI handles everything else."

The question isn't if AI will transform your workforce — it's how fast.
