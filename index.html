AI-Powered Construction Cost Analysis

How AI agents helped a general contractor identify Rp 1.93 Billion in potential savings across 11 construction projects.

Challenge

A general contractor submitted RAB (cost estimates) for 11 construction projects totaling Rp 7.13 Billion. The client needed to verify if the pricing was competitive — but manual review of hundreds of line items across complex BoQ files would take weeks.

Solution

We deployed AI agents to parse every BoQ file, extract all line items, benchmark each against regional market rates, and generate comparison reports — complete with gap analysis, root cause identification, and negotiation recommendations.
